EcoProjects

EcoProjects is a Discord Community about creating things under the EcoProjects name.
Originally founded by me and YouCanDream, it's used as a developer community and
devlog server to showcase and get advice from fellow devs working on Projects
relating to EcoProjects. This gig has many types of devs, including skript, java,
python, and game developers (c# & c++ developers). EcoProjects have been proud developers and partners with
the following Discord Servers, Networks, and Communities: EcoZero, ZeroDF and ZeroPrisons.
